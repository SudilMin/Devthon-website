About Dev{thon} 3.0

Dev{thon} 3.0 is an exciting web competition aimed at fostering innovation and creativity among developers. Participants will have the opportunity to showcase their skills, collaborate with peers, and create impactful projects.

Our Goals

The primary goal of Dev{thon} 3.0 is to encourage teamwork and problem-solving through coding. We aim to provide a platform for developers to learn, grow, and connect with industry professionals.

History of Dev{thon}

Dev{thon} 1.0

Dev{thon} 1.0 was launched in 2021, bringing together a diverse group of developers to tackle real-world challenges. The event was a great success, with numerous innovative projects being developed.

Dev{thon} 2.0

In 2022, Dev{thon} 2.0 expanded its reach, attracting even more participants and sponsors. The competition featured workshops, mentorship, and networking opportunities, further enhancing the experience for all involved.
